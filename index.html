About the Club

Crescentia is the official Computer Science and Engineering club of our institution. Founded by passionate students and supported by experienced faculty, Crescentia stands as a platform for creativity, collaboration, and career-building in the world of technology.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world skills through workshops, competitions, coding challenges, and tech talks. We believe in building a strong technical community that supports every student in their growth journey — whether they’re exploring AI, developing websites, solving problems, or managing events.

We conduct regular technical and non-technical events, offering something for everyone — from hackathons and coding contests to debates, quizzes, and design challenges.
